Submission Guidelines

Before submitting your work, please review our guidelines:

  • All submissions must be original work created by Summit Technical College students
  • Written submissions should be between 300-1500 words
  • Images should be high resolution (minimum 1200px width)
  • Include your full name, Module, and contact information
  • Allow 2-3 weeks for our editorial team to review your submission

If you have any questions, please contact our editorial team at magazine@summittech.edu
